Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of dough baked with a variety of toppings.
Traditional pizza is usually topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh herbs, creating a
rich and savory flavor. The crust can be thin and crispy or soft and thick depending on the style of
pizza. It is enjoyed all around the world and has become one of the most loved comfort foods.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Onions
  • Capsicum
  • Mushrooms
  • Oregano
  • Chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll the pizza dough into a round shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, onions, and olives.
  6. Sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes for extra flavor.
  7. Bake the pizza in the oven for 12-15 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
