Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a delicious sandwich made with a grilled beef patty, melted cheese, and fresh
vegetables placed inside a soft burger bun. It is one of the most popular fast foods in the world
and is loved for its juicy flavor and satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Beef patty
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Heat a pan or grill over medium heat.
  2. Cook the beef patty until fully browned.
  3. Place cheese on the patty and let it melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, and onion to the bun.
  6. Place the cheesy patty inside the bun.
  7. Add ketchup and mayonnaise.
  8. Serve the cheeseburger hot.
